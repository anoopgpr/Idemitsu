FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

Mobile Connectivity Issue


My app crashed or shows error screen?

Close the app from "Force Stop" and restart the mobile application.

I have Low connectivity?

Check the mobile internet connection, restart the mobile device to reset internet connection.

I cannot access or log in to the Idemitsu India App?

Check your internet connection, check if mobile number entered and OTP receieved is correct.

Use my location Data?

This is required for using all the features of the mobile application.

Login Process


How can I login?

Click on the Idemitsu India mobile app. You will land on the Idemitsu India mobile app login screen.

How to create a login account?

Click on the Idemitsu India mobile app. You will land on the Idemitsu India mobile app login screen.After entering the mobile number and OTP for login, user will be taken to Customer Category screen, please select the appropriate Customer category and fill the registration form applicable. On successful submission of the registration form you can use the mobile application.

Which mobile number do I need to enter to login?

You can login through your registered mobile number. Kindly note, all the notification messages and OTP (One Time Password) will be sent to the mobile number you will enter to create your account.

Where will I get the OTP?

You will receive your OTP (One Time Password) on your registered mobile number which you entered while creating new login account.

Where can I check my registered mobile number?

Registered mobile number is available on the "Membership" card displayed in the Side Bar menu.

Change or Update User Profile


I want to update my profile?

Use "Profile" button on available on the bottom of the home screen.

How to change my Customer Type?

Yes, in case you have registered under a wrong Customer Category, please contact the customer support. The current account will be purged and then a new user account can be created through the same number. All transaction history, any pending scheme will be removed in case of such change. User can download the transaction history for future reference.

How do I enter my email address?

Go to "Passbook" section on the bottom of the mobile app home screen.

How can I change my communication settings?

From "Profile", address can be updated in case of change. User GST and PAN can be updated directly from the applciation

How can I change my GST & PAN?

In case user has entered wrong GST or PAN number, please email the correct documents to the email provided. Customer service will update the GST and will inform the status change. Please provide 5-7 working days for any such change. User redemption will be blocked till the time of correction in the mobile application.

I want to delete my account?

User account can be deleted through Customer Care, all transaction data will be deleted.

How do I choose my profile picture in the Idemitsu India App?

On the top left corner of the Side-bar menu, user can add any profile picture based on his/her preference.

QR Code (Coupon) Related


Where can I find the QR Code?

QR code coupon is loacated in the vade under the cap of the bottle purchased in genaral. For IRG5 10W-30 MB pack, coupon is available for scratch on the bottle sticker.

How do I scan my Coupon QR code?

Scan your QR code coupon with the red Scan QR button in the bootom middle of the home screen

What if my QR code is damaged and I am not able to scan my coupon?

Do not panic at all if your QR code is damaged or your are not able to scan your coupon. You can add your 10 character alphanumeric QR number printer on the coupon manually in the "Enter Code" Section in the Coupn Scan section

Redemption/Transaction Related


What is the value of 1 point that I receive?

1 point is eual to 1 INR

Where will I find my available Point details?

Go to "Passbook" section on the bottom of the mobile app home screen.

How can I check my Coupon Scan History?

Check "Earned History" in the passbook section, for offiline download the transaction history file.

How can I check my Redemption Transaction History?

Check "Redeem History" in the passbook section, for offiline download the transaction history file.

I am missing transactions in Transaction History?

In "Earned History" section, each coupon will come with scan date, if scan is not completed the Coupon will have pending comment, and if scan is failed the Coupon will be removed.

My points balance has not been updated?

In "Earned History" section, please check if the QR code number is appearing or not, if not scan the coupon again. If the scan is not happening contact the customer care.

I see unauthorised transactions in my transaction history?

Check your redemption point if the money is received in your account or not, if not please contact the Customer support.

Do the points remain in my account post reinstalling the app?

Yes, app re-installtion or update do not remove any data from your account.

What are the different ways to get my points redeemed?

Point can be redeemed either through Paytm Wallet or through UPI ID in your Bank Account.

What mobile number should I put in PayTM Wallet?

You can put any Paytm Wallet number, which is KYCed under your name and PAN-card. This will help in easy tax claim in case of any TDS deduction under section 194R.

How can I change my PayTM Wallet account?

Go to Redeem Points section, and you can easily update the mobile number for redemption.

What is Bank UPI ID?

This is the Bank UPI ID created with the bank to receive any money in your bank account. Neither Idemitsu nor Paytm will ever ask for any OTP /Passcode for any payment in your account. Please do not share any such infromation.

Where should I updated my Bank UPI ID?

Go to Redeem Points section, and you can easily update the Bank UPI ID.
